SCOA POWER (SPG)

SCOA POWER specializes in the supply, construction, installation and maintenance of FG Wilson GENERATION SET.

It is one of the largest suppliers of Generators in Nigeria and has been servicing the market since 1926.

We supply the following range of generators. You can click on any of the links below to view the Specifications

  • 5.5 to 22 kVA Diesel
  • 26 to 200 kVA Diesel
  • 200 to 275 kVA Diesel
  • 250 to 350 kVA Diesel
  • 350 to 750 kVA Diesel
  • 455 to 750 kVA Diesel
  • 730 to 2,500 kVA Diesel

SCOA Power has entered into partnerships with some of the largest power equipment producers and operators in the world. We specialise in all facets of the solution from :-

Feasibility Stage

Environmental Impact Assessment

Grid Evacuation Studies

Turnkey solutions from site clearing to Commissioning of the plant.

We specialise in :

Gas Fired Turbines (SingleCycle or Combined Cycle Systems)

Hydro Power Plants

Solar Powered Generation Systems.

Rural Electrification, turnkey solutions for Independent Power Producers, Supply of High Tension (HT) and Low Voltage (LV) equipment, and Installation with all necessary accessories.

Operations and Maintenance : We have a pool of qualified and experienced engineers that have a very good understanding of the African climate and can offer this service.

WHY CHOOSE US?

SCOA POWER has a long history in the industry and understand the market segments that we are involved in, this is one of the reasons that makes us one of the leading companies in West Africa today. We pride ourselves on after sales service and have a fully equipped team dedicated to external servicing and repairs.
