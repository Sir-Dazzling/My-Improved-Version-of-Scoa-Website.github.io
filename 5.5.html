The 5.5 – 22 kVA range of generator sets is designed to power your every need.

  • 26 to 200 kVA Diesel
  • 200 to 275 kVA Diesel
  • 250 to 350 kVA Diesel
  • 350 to 750 kVA Diesel
  • 455 to 750 kVA Diesel

They are suitable for use in diverse applications including construction, residential, retail and telecommunications, this range has been redesigned for optimum performance in some of the harshest and most remote environments. The common sense component arrangement and 1,000 hour service interval option ensure these models remain the telecoms technician's choice with hassle free servicing and maintenance.

Complementing our metal enclosure offering, the contemporary design of the new polymer enclosure option redefines durability in the marketplace with a corrosion proof, impact resistant material that is capable of withstanding temperatures from -40ºC to 110ºC.
